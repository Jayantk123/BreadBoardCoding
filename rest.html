Creating Developers of new Tech Era

Our Bread Board Coding Club provides a place for the introduction of cohort study regarding principles and practices that are the foundation of understanding how coding and electronics support each other as well as how they work. With a fast changing world with an increase in demand for Coding Skills, our intention is to provide everything students need to explore their ideas, develop new skills related to the field of computers. We also help in building confidence in students with the help of various programs and we promote the students with their ability for invention and innovation.


The club provides a strong platform for the engineering students to practically implement their innovative ideas in computers for different applications. The Club offers indispensable guidance, workshops and organizes various events for students to explore their full potential and all-round development of the students and to enrich their technical knowledge.


This Club consists of computer enthusiasts and technically minded hobbyists as the executive members. The main vision of the club is to expose the students to the latest advancements in computer technology and give individuals an opportunity for sharing and extending ideas, views and knowledge in the field of computers and fast changing technology.

Objectives of Breadboard Coding Club:

  • To provide coding skills, knowledge and mentored training.
  • To organize technical and professional programs in the college.
  • To nurture the minds of students towards coding and steering and their talents towards high quality research in different areas of Computer Engineering and Technology.
  • To make the student able converts their ideas into innovation and theoretical knowledge in practical work.
  • To let our students excel in the area of computers and cognitive sciences and produce high quality engineers having self confidence in the nation.
  • To develop fellowship between the students of our college along with the development of mutual understanding.
  • To enhance the professional development of our students, and networking among technical professionals.

Eligibility Criteria to Join Society:

Students must be enrolled in BTECH/BE Program in any branch in an AICTE/UGC Recognized college or University.

Students who want to be a Member/Mentor of the society can apply for the same. Our core team will be interviewing the candidate and if found eligible you can be a part of the society.

For a learner no interview is required. You can still join society as a learner.
